Features

Everything orbits around your sound.

No clutter, no upsells — just the tools a serious listener actually reaches for.

🎧

Background Playback

Keep listening while you browse, message, or lock your screen. Gravity never lets go of the thread.

🎨

Dynamic Themes

Every screen takes its color from the album art playing — your library becomes its own ambient light.

⬇

Offline Downloads

Save tracks and playlists to your device. No connection, no interruption.

🧠

Made For You

On-device mixes built from your listening history — Artist Mixes, Discovery, Repeat Rewind, Throwback, and Your Favorites. No account, no cloud profile.

📥

Playlist Import

Import from Spotify or Apple Music links. Your collection, matched to YouTube — no rebuild from scratch.

🎵

Synced Lyrics

Word-by-word lyrics that scroll with the music, auto-fetched the moment a track starts.

Library

A library that feels like yours.

Liked songs, downloads, playlists — one place.

Everything you've collected lives together: liked tracks, offline saves, and every playlist you've made or imported. Nothing buried, nothing locked behind tabs you don't need.

Privacy

Your device.
Your privacy.

🔒

No Account Required

No forced login. No user profiles. Open the app and start listening.

🛡

No External Servers

Search, recommendations, and mixes run entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone.

⚡

Open Source

Transparent and community-driven, line by line, on GitHub.
